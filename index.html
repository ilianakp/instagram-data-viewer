instagram-surveillance-report v1.0 -- local, private, browser-based
---------------------------------------------------------------
 
whoami
This tool reads your Instagram data export and reveals the
advertising profile built from your activity, the categories
you've been assigned, and the companies tracking you off-app.
 
privacy
Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is uploaded
to any server. Your data never leaves your computer.
 
requirements
1. Your Instagram data export in HTML format.
Settings > Your Activity > Download Your Information > HTML
2. A Chromium browser (Chrome, Edge, Brave, Opera).
 
! Browser not supported
This site requires the File System Access API.
Please open it in Chrome, Edge, Brave, or Opera.
